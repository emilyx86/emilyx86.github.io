About Me:

My name is Emily Wozniak, and I'm the female (not cisgender unfortunately) version of Ken Rockwell. A passionate photographer and tech enthusiast who has a website with only basic HTML and CSS.

FAQ:

  • What is this site about? - It's a personal blog where I share my photography experiences, camera reviews, and tech tips.
  • Why is it so simple? - I prefer a minimalist approach that focuses on content rather than flashy design. Plus I want my site to be able to work on old and basic computers.
  • How do I contact you? - You can reach me through the Contact page.
  • Do you accept guest posts? - No, I only write about my own experiences and insights.
  • Can I use your photos? - Yes, if you contact me for licencing rights.

This site is 100% real-world use. I write about what I actually shoot. No corporate sponsors, no fluff.
